SKETCHBOOKS

Eli D. Marburger

Welcome to the site of My Sketchbooks. Here you can view all of the drawings that I have accumulated during my college career. My drawing are a very big part of myself and hold much of my past thoughts, believes, and desires. There has been much time invested in working on the drawings in these books which is why they are so valuable to me.

In my work I do my best to broaden my skill and not form any one certain styles. You can see this in the diversity of my techniques and mediums. Despite this I have always had a certain efinity for pen and ink and it has become my default medium. So please feel free to look through and hopefully you will learn more about illustration, myself, and maybe even yourself.

DESIGN ONE
